## One-Sentence Takeaway

Three foundational technical-analysis concepts — ascending triangle breakouts with volume confirmation, support/resistance role reversal, and RSI bearish divergence — are illustrated with a BTC/USD example.

## Trading-Relevant Implications

- Volume confirmation elevates breakout reliability; low-volume breakouts from ascending triangles are more prone to failure.
- Role reversal provides a lower-risk re-entry opportunity after a breakout — wait for pullback to former resistance, confirm it holds as support before entering.
- Bearish RSI divergence is a warning signal for long holders, not a standalone short trigger; it works best when corroborated by price structure (e.g., failure to set new highs, break of uptrend).
