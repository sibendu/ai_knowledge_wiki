# Barbara Rockefeller

Author of *Technical Analysis For Dummies*, 4th Edition (2020, John Wiley & Sons, ISBN 978-1-119-59655-4) and earlier editions.

Known facts from the source:
- Interviewed by *Technical Analysis of Stocks and Commodities* magazine, December 2005 issue
- Has used Metastock charting software (by Equis) since the early 1980s — implies 40+ years of TA experience as of 2020
- Personally uses Fibonacci retracements (noted they took ~20 years to fully grasp — author anecdote, source: TA4D 2020)
- Publishes financial newsletters and commentary (implied by Appendix references to her own work)
- Describes herself as "not math-competent" but emphasizes visual/pattern-based TA approach

## Primary Work

- *Technical Analysis For Dummies*, 4th Edition (2020)
