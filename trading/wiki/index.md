- [RSI](indicators/rsi.md) - Relative Strength Index: momentum oscillator, overbought/oversold levels, divergence signals.
- [Breakout After Normal Reaction](setups/breakout-after-normal-reaction.md) - Setup based on recovery from orderly pullbacks.
- [Ascending Triangle Breakout](setups/ascending-triangle-breakout.md) - Flat-resistance breakout with volume confirmation and RSI filter.
- [Support and Resistance](concepts/support-resistance.md) - Price floors/ceilings and role-reversal dynamics after breakouts.
- [Stage Analysis](concepts/stage-analysis.md) - Four-stage stock price lifecycle: Neglect, Advancing, Topping, Declining.
- [Trend Template](concepts/trend-template.md) - Minervini's 8-criterion filter for confirming Stage 2 uptrends before entry.
- [Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)](setups/volatility-contraction-pattern.md) - Progressively tighter pullbacks with contracting volume forming a precise entry point.
- [Relative Strength Ranking](indicators/relative-strength-ranking.md) - IBD RS Rating: stock's 12-month return vs. all other stocks, expressed 1–99. Distinct from the RSI oscillator.
- [Earnings Acceleration](concepts/earnings-acceleration.md) - EPS growth rate increasing quarter-over-quarter; present in ~90% of the biggest historical winners.
- [Earnings Quality](concepts/earnings-quality.md) - Multi-dimensional earnings assessment: margins, Code 33, post-earnings drift, inventory/receivables signals.
- [SEPA Strategy](strategies/sepa-strategy.md) - Minervini's Specific Entry Point Analysis: convergence of trend, fundamentals, RS, VCP, and market conditions.
- [Line of Least Resistance](concepts/line-of-least-resistance.md) - Livermore's directional-break concept: trade the direction price reveals once a range breaks.
- [Tape Reading](concepts/tape-reading.md) - Inferring price direction from the behaviour of price and volume rather than news or tips.
- [Stock Group Behavior](concepts/stock-group-behavior.md) - Reading a stock against its group; the laggard-divergence warning signal.
- [Market Manipulation](concepts/market-manipulation.md) - Historical (pre-SEC) distribution mechanics and the durable inside-vs-outside-buying lesson.
- [Moving Averages](indicators/moving-averages.md) — SMA, EMA, dual/triple crossover systems, ribbon, convergence/divergence.
- [MACD](indicators/macd.md) — Moving Average Convergence Divergence: 12/26 EMA, 9-day signal line, histogram, divergence signals.
- [Bollinger Bands](indicators/bollinger-bands.md) — Volatility bands at ±2 std dev from 20-day SMA; squeeze signals impending breakout.
- [Average True Range (ATR)](indicators/average-true-range.md) — True range volatility indicator; used for stop sizing and orderliness assessment.
- [Stochastic Oscillator](indicators/stochastic-oscillator.md) — %K/%D momentum oscillator using H, L, C; overbought/oversold and divergence signals.
- [TA4D Pivot Points](indicators/ta4d-pivot-points.md) — Mathematical PP=(H+L+C)/3 formula with R1-R3/S1-S3 zones; day-trading S/R. Distinct from Livermore's Pivotal Point.
- [Dow Theory](concepts/dow-theory.md) — Foundational TA: prices trend, primary trends, secondary retracements, persistence until disrupted.
- [Price Bars](concepts/price-bars.md) — OHLC bar construction, bar scoring, special bars, gaps taxonomy (breakaway/runaway/exhaustion/island).
- [Candlestick Charting](concepts/candlestick-charting.md) — Japanese candlestick anatomy, doji types, shaven bars, multi-bar emotional patterns.
- [Chart Patterns](concepts/chart-patterns.md) — Continuation patterns (triangles, flags) and reversal patterns (H&S, double top/bottom); measured moves.
- [Trendlines and Channels](concepts/trendlines-channels.md) — Rule-based trendlines, regression channels, breakout authentication, orderliness, blowout tops.
- [Volume Analysis](concepts/volume-analysis.md) — OBV, volume divergence, volume spikes, volume-price confirmation rules.
- [Ichimoku](concepts/ichimoku.md) — Hosoda's five-component system: tenkan/kijun, senkou spans, chikou, kumo cloud.
- [Point and Figure](concepts/point-and-figure.md) — X/O chart format filtering time/noise; vertical and horizontal price projections.
