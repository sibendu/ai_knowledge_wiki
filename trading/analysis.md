## Methodology Notes

**Livermore Pivotal Points** are price levels where the market demonstrates clear directional conviction — a swing high or swing low at which price reversal indicates a change in the line of least resistance. In this analysis they are identified as the highest high (or lowest low) within a ±10-day window, then sequenced to reveal the underlying rhythm of accumulation, markup, distribution, and decline phases.

**Trend Template** (8-criteria check):
1. Price > MA50
2. Price > MA150
3. Price > MA200
4. MA50 > MA150
5. MA150 > MA200
6. Price ≥ 25% above 52-week low
7. Price within 25% of 52-week high
8. Positive 1-month momentum

**Swing Opportunity Rating:** Strong Setup / Conditional / Watchlist / Avoid
